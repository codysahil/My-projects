Space Company

GSLV MK 3

The GSLV MK 3 is a heavy lift launch vehicle developed by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). It has the capability to launch satellites weighing up to 4 tons into the Geosynchronous Transfer Orbit (GTO).

Falcon 9

Falcon 9 is a reusable rocket developed and manufactured by SpaceX. It has the capability to launch payloads weighing up to 22.8 tons into Low Earth Orbit (LEO).

Space Competition

With the increasing interest in space exploration and the increasing demand for satellite launches, there has been a growing competition among space companies. Major players in the industry include SpaceX, Blue Origin, and Arianespace. The competition is not only limited to launches but also extends to areas like reusable rockets, interplanetary missions, and the establishment of a permanent human presence in space.
